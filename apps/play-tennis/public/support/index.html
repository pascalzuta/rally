Frequently Asked Questions

Sign up and select your county during registration. Then join your county's lobby from the Home tab. When 6 or more players have joined, a round-robin tournament is automatically created and match scheduling begins.
Rally analyzes all players' availability and finds overlapping 2-hour windows. Matches are categorized as: auto-scheduled (confirmed time both players are free), needs-accept (proposed time one player needs to confirm), or needs-negotiation (no overlap found — players coordinate directly via in-app messaging).
Go to the Tournament tab, find your match, and tap to expand it. Enter the set scores and submit. Your opponent will be notified and asked to confirm the result. Once both players agree, the scores are finalized and ratings are updated.
Rally Rating is an ELO-based system that measures your competitive level. After each match, your rating adjusts based on whether you won or lost, and the strength of your opponent. Beating a higher-rated player earns more points than beating a lower-rated one. Your rating appears on your county leaderboard.
