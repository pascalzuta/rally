Structure

Transparency and alignment by design.

Green Room Partners is a California registered investment advisor (RIA) managing capital via separately managed accounts (SMAs). All investment accounts are held in clients’ names, allowing full transparency and individual tax optimization.

We are investors

We recommend investing assets with at least a three-year timeframe and offer two long-term opportunity sets: a traditional investment account and a self-directed Individual Retirement Account (IRA).

Alignment

Our structure is designed for alignment: transparent ownership, consistent process, and long-duration decision quality.

Long-term first

We are investors, not traders, and we target clients who share a long-duration mindset.
